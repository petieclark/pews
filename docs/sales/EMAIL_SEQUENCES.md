## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- **Keep it short** (40-50 characters max for mobile)
- **Ask a question** (e.g., "How much are you paying for church software?")
- **Use numbers** (e.g., "$2,400/year saved")
- **Create curiosity** (e.g., "Are you copying data between systems?")
- **Avoid spam triggers** ("FREE," "ACT NOW," "LIMITED TIME")

### Body Copy
- **Personalize** (use church name, pastor name, specific pain points from discovery)
- **Keep it short** (150-250 words max)
- **One CTA per email** (don't ask them to do 5 things—pick one)
- **Write like a human** (not corporate jargon)
- **Break up text** (short paragraphs, bullet points, bold key phrases)

### Timing
- **Send during business hours** (9 AM - 5 PM local time, Tuesday-Thursday)
- **Avoid Sundays** (church staff are busy!)
- **Avoid Mondays** (inboxes are flooded)
- **Avoid Fridays** (people are checked out)

### CTAs (Calls to Action)
- **Make it easy** ([Book a Demo], [Subscribe Now])
- **Remove friction** (one-click calendar link, no forms to fill out)
- **Offer multiple paths** ("Book a demo" or "Reply with your availability")

## Tracking & Optimization

### Metrics to Track
- **Open rate** (Goal: 40%+)
- **Reply rate** (Goal: 10%+)
- **Demo booking rate** (Goal: 5%+)
- **Conversion rate** (Goal: 20-30% of demos → customers)

### A/B Testing Ideas
- Subject line variations (question vs. statement)
- Opening line (cost question vs. pain point)
- CTA placement (top vs. bottom)
- Length (short vs. long)

### When to Stop Sending
- They explicitly say "Not interested"
- They unsubscribe
- After 3 emails with no response (cold sequence)
- After 3 emails with no response (post-demo)

## CRM Tagging

Use these tags to organize leads and automate sequences:

| Tag | Meaning | Next Action |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| `lead-cold` | Never contacted | Send cold outreach sequence |
| `lead-warm` | Expressed interest, no demo yet | Send demo invite |
| `demo-scheduled` | Demo booked | Send reminder, prep call |
| `demo-completed` | Demo done | Send post-demo sequence |
| `trial-active` | Using trial | Send check-in emails |
| `trial-ending` | Trial ends in 3 days | Send conversion sequence |
| `trial-ended` | Trial expired, no conversion | Re-engagement in 3-6 months |
| `customer` | Paying customer | Remove from sales sequences |
| `disqualified` | Not a fit | Stop emailing |

## Sample Cold Outreach List

Where to find cold leads:

1. **Denomination directories** (Baptist, Methodist, etc. publish member church lists)
2. **Google Maps** (search "churches near [city]")
3. **Church websites** (look for "Contact" or "Staff" page to find admin email)
4. **LinkedIn** (search "church administrator" or "executive pastor")
5. **Facebook groups** (join church leadership groups, engage authentically)
6. **Referrals** (ask existing customers for intros)

## Legal & Compliance

### CAN-SPAM Compliance
- ✅ Include unsubscribe link in every email
- ✅ Include physical address in footer
- ✅ Honor opt-outs within 10 business days
- ✅ Don't use deceptive subject lines

### GDPR (if emailing EU churches)
- ✅ Get explicit consent before emailing
- ✅ Allow data export/deletion on request
- ✅ Include privacy policy link
