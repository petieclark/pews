## Competitor Breakdown

### Planning Center (PCO)
**Website**: https://www.planningcenter.com

Planning Center is the market leader, but it's expensive and modular—each feature is a separate product with separate pricing.

#### Pricing Structure
Planning Center uses a **per-product model**. You pay separately for each module:

| Product | Base Price | Scale Pricing |
|---------|-----------|---------------|
| **People** (Member directory, households) | $20/mo for 100 people | +$15/mo per 100 additional |
| **Check-Ins** (Kids check-in, labels, security) | $20/mo | +$15/mo per 100 attendees |
| **Giving** (Online donations, recurring gifts) | 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction | +$19/mo for ACH/bank transfers |
| **Groups** (Small groups, signups) | $20/mo | +$10/mo per 100 members |
| **Services** (Worship planning, scheduling) | $20/mo | +$10/mo per service |
| **Publishing** (Church app, website) | $29/mo | N/A |

#### Example Church Costs

**Small Church (150 members, 100 weekly attendance)**:
- People: $20
- Check-Ins: $20
- Giving: 2.9% of donations (~$100-150/mo on $4,000 giving) + $19 for ACH
- Groups: $20
- **Total: ~$230-280/month**

**Mid-Size Church (400 members, 250 weekly attendance)**:
- People: $20 + $45 (300 extra people) = $65
- Check-Ins: $20 + $30 (150 extra attendees) = $50
- Giving: 2.9% of donations (~$250-350/mo on $10,000 giving) + $19 for ACH
- Groups: $20 + $30 (300 extra) = $50
- Services: $20
- **Total: ~$500-650/month**

**Large Church (1,000 members, 600 weekly attendance)**:
- People: $20 + $135 (900 extra) = $155
- Check-Ins: $20 + $75 (500 extra) = $95
- Giving: 2.9% of donations (~$600-800/mo on $25,000 giving) + $19 for ACH
- Groups: $20 + $90 (900 extra) = $110
- Services: $20 + $50 = $70
- Publishing: $29
- **Total: ~$1,100-1,300/month**

#### What You DON'T Get:
- No built-in SMS communication (requires Twilio integration + costs)
- No email marketing (must use separate tool like Mailchimp)
- No drip campaigns
- No financial reporting beyond giving (need separate accounting tool)
- Limited automation

### Tithe.ly
**Website**: https://get.tithe.ly

Tithe.ly is a **giving-focused platform** that also offers church management features. Many churches use it alongside PCO or Breeze.

#### Pricing (Giving Only)

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Transaction Fees |
|------|-------------|------------------|
| **Free Plan** | $0 | 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction |
| **Standard Plan** | $19/mo | 2.9% + $0.30 (no change) |

**Wait, why pay for Standard or Pro if fees are the same?**  
Higher tiers unlock:
- Custom branding (remove Tithe.ly logo)
- Text-to-give (SMS keyword donations)
- Recurring giving management
- Donor CRM (track donor history, engagement)

#### Full ChMS (Tithe.ly People)

If you want Tithe.ly's church management features (people, groups, check-in), pricing jumps:

| Church Size | Monthly Cost |
|-------------|-------------|
| Up to 100 people | $39/mo |
| Up to 250 people | $69/mo |
| Up to 500 people | $129/mo |
| Up to 1,000 people | $229/mo |
| 1,000+ people | Custom pricing |

#### What's Included:
- Giving (online, mobile app, text-to-give, kiosk)
- People directory
- Attendance tracking
- Groups and events
- Basic check-in (not as robust as PCO)
- Email/SMS (limited sends per month)

#### Drawbacks:
- **Transaction fees never decrease** (2.9% forever, even at scale)
- **Check-in is basic** (no household view, limited customization)
- **SMS limits** (must pay for extra sends beyond plan limits)
- **No volunteer scheduling** (must use separate tool)

## The Multi-Tool Problem

Most churches don't use just one tool. They cobble together a stack:

### Example: Typical Church Software Stack

| Tool | Purpose | Monthly Cost |
|------|---------|--------------|
| Planning Center People | Member directory | $65 |
| Planning Center Check-Ins | Kids check-in | $50 |
| Tithe.ly | Online giving | $69 + 2.9% fees (~$120 total) |
| Mailchimp | Email newsletters | $50 (500+ contacts) |
| Google Forms | Event signups | Free (but manual data entry) |
| **TOTAL** | | **~$350/month** |

### Problems With This Approach:
1. **Data silos**: People data in PCO, giving data in Tithe.ly, email lists in Mailchimp
2. **Manual syncing**: Copy/paste between systems (error-prone, time-consuming)
3. **Duplicate payments**: Paying for overlapping features
4. **Training complexity**: Staff and volunteers must learn multiple tools
5. **No unified reporting**: Can't see full picture of member engagement

## Why Pews is Cheaper

### 1. Modern Tech Stack
- **Planning Center** was built in 2006 (Ruby on Rails monolith)
- **Gloo** acquired legacy code from FellowshipOne (2000s-era .NET)
- **Pews** built in 2024 with modern tooling:
  - **Supabase** (serverless PostgreSQL, real-time sync)
  - **Next.js** (React-based, fast, mobile-first)
  - **Edge hosting** (Vercel global CDN, no server costs)

**Result**: 10x lower infrastructure costs → passed to customers.

### 2. No Enterprise Bloat
- No sales team (cold calls, demos, negotiations)
- No account managers (one-size-fits-all pricing)

### 3. One Codebase, One Product
- Planning Center maintains **7 separate products** (People, Check-Ins, Giving, Groups, Services, Publishing, Registrations)

### 4. Transparent Pricing
