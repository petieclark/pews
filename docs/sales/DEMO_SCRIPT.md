## Tips for a Great Demo

1. **Move fast**: 30 minutes goes quickly. Don't get bogged down in edge cases.
2. **Let them drive**: Ask what they want to see. Adapt the script.
3. **Tell stories**: "Here's how one church used this to re-engage 40 inactive families..."
4. **Show, don't tell**: Click, type, demo. Not slides.
5. **Handle objections with empathy**: "I get it. Change is hard. Here's how we make it easy..."
6. **End with clarity**: What's the next step? Who's making the decision? When?
